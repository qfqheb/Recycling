How It Works

Step 1: Schedule a Pickup

Sign in to your account and schedule a convenient pickup time for your plastic waste.

Step 2: Prepare Your Waste

Gather your plastic waste and ensure it's properly separated and ready for pickup.

Step 3: Pickup Service

Our team will arrive at your location and collect the plastic waste for recycling.

Step 4: Recycling Process

We partner with local recycling centers to ensure your plastic waste is recycled properly.
